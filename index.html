About Industry Day

 

We would like to request your participation in IIT Delhi first ever Industry Day. IIT Delhi has been working actively with the industries over several decades. More than 350 industrial projects with over 130 Corporates have been ventured in the last 5 years alone. This event aims to build on the strong relationship we already enjoy with the industry and further amplify it.

 

IITD Industry day aims to:

 

-Enhance industry visibility of cutting edge research at IITD.

 

-Enhance IITD's visibility into industrial problems.

 

-Generate new IITD-Industry collaborations.

 

-Showcase existing IITD-Industry relationships.
